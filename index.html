About Me

I believe the danger isn't that AI will disobey us, but that it will obey our flaws too perfectly. My work - from engineering "digital masks" for marginalized Roma youth to preserving UNESCO heritage sites - is driven by "algorithmic courage": the will to build systems that protect history and reject stereotypes.


Growing up near the Baku railway, I developed "industrial-grade" focus and a respect for invisible labor. I am a systems thinker who enters projects not just with action, but with understanding.


Fun fact: I am a real-life "Waldo" - I somehow accidentally appear in the background of strangers' photos constantly.
